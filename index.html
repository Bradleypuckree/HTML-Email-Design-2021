SYSPRO R1 2021 is now available
SYSPRO continues to evolve and bring solutions that improve the efficiency of your customer’s business and increase overall performance. The first release of SYSPRO 8 for 2021 is now available. New capabilities have been introduced to various modules including manufacturing, distribution, point of sale, finance and Avanti. Some of these include:
  1. Improved order picking in SYSPRO Espresso – update orders in real-time
  2. Flexible consolidated invoice pricing – specify which price to use instead of selecting defaults
  3. Barcoding scanning support added – optimize search of stock items
  4. Bill of Material cost implosion in SQL – optimize the operations process
  5. New Accounts Receivables and Accounts Payable group payments – allowing processing of a single payment against multiple customers or suppliers
